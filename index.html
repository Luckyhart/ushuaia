Brand Strategy Proposal
Powered by the wind.
Guided by purpose.
A new positioning for Ushuaia Shipping
The strategic shift

Philosophy first.
Port agency second.

The goal is to be recognised as a wind-powered, net zero pioneer first, and a port agency second. The services don't change. The identity does.

A wind turbine powers the office. The vehicle fleet is transitioning to fully electric. These aren't future promises — they are present actions that no competitor in Ushuaia can match.

"We are on a mission to become the world's first net zero expedition port agency."

Brand phrases

The slogans

Primary — global brand statement
Powered by the wind.
Guided by purpose.
Unambiguous, active, positive. Works for every audience — expedition operators, sustainability directors, new contacts. No shadow meaning. Pure forward momentum.
Lives on: hero headline · jacket backs · caps · email signatures · pitch decks · all external comms
Secondary — credentials shorthand
Wind powered. Net zero.
The fast scan version. Two beats. Works as a nav bar tag, collar label, footer line or email signature suffix.
Lives on: nav bar · footer · email signatures · clothing labels · business cards
Contextual — expedition operators & industry events
Where the world ends,
our commitment begins.
For audiences who know Ushuaia and Fin del Mundo. Used selectively — powerful at IAATO and in proposals to expedition operators who immediately recognise the geography.
Lives on: sustainability page · tote bag · industry conference materials · operator proposals
Insider — merchandise & gifts
54° South.
Ushuaia's latitude. No explanation needed for anyone in the expedition world. Always starts a conversation with the right people.
Lives on: beanie side · cap embroidery · conference gifts
